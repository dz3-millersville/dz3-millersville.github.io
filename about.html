Student at Millersville University

My name is Dan Zalewski and I'm a student at Millersville University. My primary focus for the last 10 years has been on videography, graphic communications, and marketing. I'm currently wrapping up my Bachelors of Science in Communication with a focus in Broadcasting. I'm also taking an exploratory graduate course in History and am considering a Masters in History to compliment my interests and ultimate goal of creating professional and academically sound video content on historical subjects.

Beyond the University, I currently hold two professional positions. The first is as a manager of a niche game retailer where I promote the advancement of marketing strategy, communication standards, and manage the profitability of the space, alongside my team of five other talented individuals. I also work as the lead video producer for a company that assists non-profit businesses with fundraising, event planning, and marketing. In my spare time I also help manage a non-profit foundation in memory of my mother that aims to provide collegiate scholarships to students looking to pursue the arts.

Previously, I ran a video production company for 3 years. In that time, we produced content for local businesses, charities, and developed two feature length documentaries. We served clients across the eastern half of the United States, and the experience taught me a lot about the business side of videography and graphic communications.
