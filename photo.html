Digital Photography

Digital Photography is the process of capturing images by exposing a digital image sensor to light in a controlled manner. Through the use of multilayered glass lens, timing controls for shutter speed, and other digital controls for the image sensor, an experienced operator can achieve their exact desired look they want the subject of the image to have.

For my Digital Photography project I had the opportunity to capture some rather unique images. I had traveled to New England and had the chance to capture photos of the Vermont Capitol Building, the mountains and cabins of northern New Hampshire, and the proposal of two personal friends of mine.

Reflecting on my Project

Looking back on this project, I'm pretty happy with how it turned out. One of the things I felt really good about was the overall composition of the photographs. I feel like I had a solid grasp of utilizing the basic composition elements to create generally pleasing photographs.

An area of improvement would be my general overall risk taking and willingness to think outside the box. While I'm happy with the images I've included, I feel like I could've tried to take more interesting and varied photographs, especially of people. I feel like I stuck mainly with static objects because they are more consistent and easier to take time photographing.
